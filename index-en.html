GAINA TECHNOLOGY

Space Technology at Your Service

GAINA is an advanced ceramic technology (insulating paint) derived from JAXA (Japan Aerospace Exploration Agency) research, used to cover rocket nose cones to protect them from extreme heat.

"Here construction meets technology.. We use JAXA solutions to provide you with the best thermal and acoustic insulation in the world."

Advanced Nano Ceramic
500 microns thickness (Same as regular paint)
10-year warranty (Lasts over 20 years)

Revolutionary Benefits & Features

Superior Thermal Insulation

Insulates heat up to 30°C, creating a comfortable indoor environment even in harshest conditions.

Energy Saving (51%)

Maintains temperature and reduces compressor work, saving electricity costs up to 51%.

Waterproofing & Moisture

Acts as a protective shield against water leakage and humidity, fully protecting the building structure.

Fire Resistance

Fire resistant up to 1000°C for a full hour, with high safety against flame spread.

Effective Acoustic Insulation

Significantly reduces external and internal noise, providing optimal quietness.

UV & IR Resistance

Unaffected by ultraviolet or infrared rays, preserving its color and properties for decades.

Eco-Friendly

Natural non-chemical product, odorless and completely safe for health.

Odor Removal

Releases negative ions that break down and remove bad odors automatically.

Easy to Clean

Dust does not stick to it thanks to nano-technology, making maintenance and cleaning very easy.

Non-Conductive

Adds an extra safety layer by being a standard non-conductive material.

Where can you use GAINA?

Applied to all surfaces: Concrete, Iron, Fabric, Wood, Flooring, Asphalt, and even Refrigerators and Sandwich Panels.

Hospitals Hotels Warehouses Refrigerators Towers Power Stations Telecom Residential Units
